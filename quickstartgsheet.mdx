## Install the Addon

You can install GPTPro Suite add-on from the **Google Workspace Marketplace**

## Activate GPT Formulas in Google Sheets

To use the GPT Formulas, you need to activate them first.&#x20;

Click on **Extensions** >  GPTPro Suite> **Enable GPT Functions**

GPT to prompt ChatGPT from cells&#x20;

GPT\_TRANSLATE to translate your cells content&#x20;

GPT\_CLASSIFY to classify your cells content&#x20;

GPT\_EXTRACT to extract from your cells&#x20;

GPT\_SUMMARIZE to summarize your cells&#x20;

GPT\_FORMAT to sanitize or reformat your cells content&#x20;
